Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

Is TesterUp really free to join? +

Yes! TesterUp is completely free to join and use. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or costs. You earn money, not spend it.

How much can I earn? +

Earnings vary based on test complexity and time required. Testers earn rewards for completing app tests. The more tests you complete, the more you can earn.

What kind of apps will I test? +

You'll test a variety of apps including games, productivity tools, social apps, and more. All from legitimate developers looking for real user feedback before launch.

How do I get rewards? +

You can redeem your earnings for gift cards from popular retailers, enter prize draws, or receive other rewards once you reach the minimum threshold.

Is my data safe? +

Absolutely. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption, are fully GDPR compliant, and never sell your data. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

What devices are supported? +

TesterUp is available on both iOS (iPhone/iPad) and Android devices. You need iOS 12.0+ or Android 5.0+ to run the app.
